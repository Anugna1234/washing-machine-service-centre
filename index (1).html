What We Fix

Services Provided

From minor glitches to major overhauls — our certified technicians handle all types of washing machine issues.

⚡
Not Starting
Machine won't power on or fails to start its cycle — we diagnose and fix it fast.
🌀
Drum Not Spinning
Drum stops mid-cycle or doesn't rotate. We repair belts, bearings and motors.
💧
Water Leakage
Leaks from hose, door seal or pump — we stop leaks and prevent water damage.
📳
Vibration Problem
Excessive shaking during spin cycle. We level and fix suspension and balance issues.
🔊
Noise During Operation
Banging, grinding or squealing sounds fixed — bearings, drum spider and more.
🚿
Drainage Issues
Machine not draining properly? Clogged pump filters and hoses cleared quickly.
⚙️
Motor Repair
Motor burnout or failure — serviced or replaced with high-quality compatible parts.
🖥️
PCB / Control Board
Electronic faults, error codes, display issues and control board repair covered.
🔒
Door Lock Problems
Door won't open or lock — interlock mechanism repaired or replaced efficiently.
🛠️
Installation & Maintenance
New machine setup, annual servicing and preventive maintenance packages available.
